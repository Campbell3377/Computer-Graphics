Bivariate Surface Display Demo

This program demonstrates the wireframe display of a bivariate surface. Isoparametric curves on the surface are displayed -- that is the wireframe.
Z-buffer hidden surface removal and "polygon offset" are used to create the illusion that this is a pure wireframe model. The surface triangles are displayed in the background color. (This program implements triangle fans.)
The polygon offset function with the zbuffer flag LEQUAL fixes a z-fighting problem and improves the display.
